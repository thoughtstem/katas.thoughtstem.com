7.31 Farm Kata

read
Write a collection of games. The 1st game has a horse eating 5 apples, 5 grapes and 5 kiwis, collecting gold coins, and avoiding a dog. The 2nd game has a llama eating 5 apples and 5 grapes, collecting silver coins, and avoiding 2 dogs and a wolf. The 3rd game has a rabbit eating 5 apples, collecting copper coins, and avoiding 3 wolves. Close one game to continue with the next.
code
#lang k2/lang/farm/enemies
 
(start
  horse
  ((apple 5) (grapes 5) (kiwi 5))
  (gold)
  (dog))
 
(start
  llama
  ((apple 5) (grapes 5))
  (silver)
  ((dog 2) wolf))
 
(start rabbit ((apple 5)) (copper) ((wolf 3)))
