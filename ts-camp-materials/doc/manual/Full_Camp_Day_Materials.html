2 Full Camp Day Materials

All the materials needed to run a successful full-day, one day camp!

2.1 Kata Cards

# = 1 per student

Each student receives one of the following at the very beginning of the day.

Write their name and note their photo-permission status by either crossing out the camera icon or leaving it be! Give to student to slide it into a name tag holder with lanyard.

2.4 Tech

A typical full day camp will need the following tech supplies:

  • 30 chromebooks

  • 30 chargers

  • 30 mice

  • 8 power strips

  • 8 extention cords

2.5 Misc Supplies

Don’t forget to pack these too!

  • Market

  • ThoughtSTEM Dollars

  • Stickers for bonus points

  • Index cards

  • Markers

  • 5 timers

The following items might also come in handy:

  • Extra tables

  • Scrap paper

  • Combination lock for computer locker

  • Clipboards

  • Projector
